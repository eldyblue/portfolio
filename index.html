Projects

Osurnament.com

Osurnament is a both a CMS and an Esport platform for the game osu!
It's a robust realtime web application in which people can create and manage tournaments or simply participate to them with a simple SSO connexion with their osu! account.
People can also register according to certain conditions like their rank to any tournament created on the platform.

Tech stack

I built and maintain this application alone with Laravel, Livewire, Alpine and Tailwindcss.

Features:
  • A custom laravel provider for SSO connexion with the osu! API.
  • A notification system for each action happening on the application.
  • A microservices architecture to handle each step of a tournament.
  • A strong management and delegation system for the tournament's staff.
