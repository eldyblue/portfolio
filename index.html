Projects

Osurnament.com

Osurnament is a web platform designed to centralize and streamline the organization of osu! tournaments. It allows players and staff members to easily create, manage, and participate in tournaments.

Tech stack

I built and maintain this application alone with Laravel, Livewire, Alpine and Tailwindcss.

Key Features

Tournament Creation & Management

  • Supports solo and team-based tournaments.
  • Allows tournament organizers to manage registrations, brackets, and schedules.
  • Supports single and double elimination formats (for now).

User Roles & Registration

  • Players can register for tournaments individually or as part of a team.
  • Users can apply as staff members to help manage tournaments.
  • Administrators have full control over tournament approvals and participant management.

Team System

  • Players can create teams and invite others to join.
  • Teams can register for tournaments that support team-based competition.

Ranking & Points System

  • A dynamic point-based ranking system for players.
  • Only applies to tournaments that are community-approved.

Notifications & Invitations

  • Players receive notifications for tournament-related actions (invitations, registrations, etc.).
  • Organizers can send invitations and updates to participants.

Rank Restrictions

  • Tournaments can have rank limits (e.g., only players ranked between 1 and 1000).
  • Players exceeding the rank limit are automatically restricted from joining.
